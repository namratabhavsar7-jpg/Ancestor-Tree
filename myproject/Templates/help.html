Frequently Asked Questions

How do I start building my tree?

Click on "Get Started" from the Home page or "Add Member" from the sidebar. Follow the 7-step process to fill in all the details for a family member.

Can I edit records later?

Yes! Go to "View Records" in the sidebar. You will see an ✏️ icon next to each member. Click it to update their information.

How secure is my family data?

We use secure database systems and sessions to ensure your data is safe and isolated. We don't share your heritage with anyone.

How do I download my data?

Go to "View Records" and click the "📥 Export Records" button at the top right. This will download a CSV file compatible with Excel.

Still need help?

Our support team is available to assist you with any technical issues.
